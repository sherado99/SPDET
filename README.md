# Stech Email Tone Improver

Transform your cold or robotic emails into warm, human, and honest messages using Stech AI.

## Input
- `originalEmail` (required): The email content to improve.
- `targetTone` (optional): e.g., "friendly", "empathetic". Default: "warm and honest".
- `additionalInstructions` (optional): Extra guidance.

## Output
- `originalEmail`
- `improvedEmail`
- `targetTone`
- `timestamp`

## Example
Input:
> Dear Sir, your application has been rejected.

Output:
> Hi, thank you for your application. Unfortunately we couldn't move forward this time, but we appreciate your effort.
